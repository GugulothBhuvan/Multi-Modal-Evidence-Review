# 12. AI Judge Talking Points



When asked:



### Why not use agents?



Answer:



> The task is fundamentally a structured classification and verification problem. We used modular components with deterministic decisioning instead of autonomous agent loops to improve reliability, reduce hallucinations, and simplify debugging.



---



### Why separate LLM and VLM?



Answer:



> Text and visual reasoning have different failure modes. Separating them improves observability and allows independent prompt optimization.



---



### Why rules for final decision?



Answer:



> The challenge requires evidence-grounded outputs. A rule engine guarantees consistency and prevents a language model from inventing unsupported conclusions.
